#1: Bondi Beach Rescue


Bondi Beach Rescue is an Australian television program that shows the routines of the Waverly Concil Professional Lifeguards who patrol Bondi Beach. These lifeguards perform over 5,000 rescues during the summer period. They also encounter other incidents including lost children, drunk beach goers, injuries, shark scares, and thieves. Footage of the show is shot during November through Feburary, with Christmas being the busiest time of the year. Current episodes of Bondi Beach Rescue is available on YouTube and Amazon Prime Video in the United States.
