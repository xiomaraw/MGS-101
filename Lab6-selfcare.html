#3: Selfcare Saturdays

This month has been stressful with midterms and upcoming projects, I implemented a Selfcare Saturday for mental health. Whenever I find free time, I make sure I use it wisely because I never know when I'll be free again between school and virtual volunteering (see planner for week 9 below). However, I try to follow this schedule when I'm free:

  1. Clean Room
  2. Meditate
  3. Listen to lo-fi music
  4. Bake brownies or chocolate chip cookies
  5. Watch netflix (currently re-watching Grey's Anatomy) and eat brownies
  6. Light a vanilla scent candle and read
  7. Sleep for as long as I can
